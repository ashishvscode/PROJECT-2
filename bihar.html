TOP 5 TEMPLES IN BIHAR

  • MAHABODHI TEMPLE

    Located in Bodh Gaya, this UNESCO World Heritage Site is where Lord Buddha attained enlightenment under the Bodhi tree.

  • VISHNUPAD TEMPLE

    Situated in Gaya, this ancient temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and is believed to be built over the footprint of Lord Vishnu.

  • PATNA SAHIB GURUDWARA

    Located in Patna, this is one of the holiest Sikh shrines, built at the birthplace of Guru Gobind Singh, the tenth Sikh Guru.

  • MAHESHVAR DHAM TEMPLE

    Situated in Bhagalpur, this temple is dedicated to Lord Shiva and is known for its beautiful architecture and serene surroundings.

  • KALI TEMPLE

    Located in Purnia, this temple is dedicated to Goddess Kali and is a significant pilgrimage site for devotees.
