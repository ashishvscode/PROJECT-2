TOP 5 TEMPLES IN MADHYA PRADESH

  • SHRI MAHAKALESWAR JYOTRILINGA TEMPLE

    • Located in Ujjain, this is one of India's 12 sacred Jyotirlinga shrines dedicated to Lord Shiva.

  • OMKARESHWAR TEMPLE

    • Also a Jyotirlinga shrine, the Omkareshwar temple is situated on an island in the Narmada River in Omkareshwar.

  • KHAJURAHO TEMPLE

    • This group of medieval Hindu and Jain temples is famous for its intricate architecture, including the largest and most ornate temple, the Kandariya Mahadeva Temple.

  • CHAUSATH YOGINI TEMPLE

    • There are two famous Chausath Yogini temples, one near Khajuraho and another in Bhedaghat, Jabalpur, with the latter being a historic 10th-century structure dedicated to Goddess Durga.

  • KAL BHAIRAV TEMPLE

    • Another significant temple in Ujjain, it is dedicated to Kal Bhairav, the guardian deity of Ujjain, and is notable for being open 24 hours.
