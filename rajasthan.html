TOP 5 TEMPLES IN RAJASTHAN

  • DILWARA TEMPLES

    • Located on Mount Abu, these 11th to 13th-century Jain temples are famous for their stunning, intricate marble carvings.

  • BRAHMA TEMPLE

    • (Pushkar): Situated in the sacred town of Pushkar, this is one of the few temples in the world dedicated to Lord Brahma and features a unique red spire and silver doors.

  • KARNI MATA TEMPLE

    • This famous temple in Deshnoke is known for the thousands of rats, or "kabas," that reside there and are considered sacred by devotees.

  • BIRLA TEMPLE

    • (Jaipur): A modern architectural marvel, this beautiful temple is dedicated to Lord Vishnu and Goddess Lakshmi and is made entirely of white marble.

  • MEHANDIPUR BALAJI TEMPLE

    • This popular temple, devoted to Lord Hanuman, is known for its strong religious significance and is a prominent pilgrimage site in Rajasthan.
